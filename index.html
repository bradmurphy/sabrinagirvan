my expertise

For 15 years I have experienced the amazing results in what I feel is the way to attain optimum health. At 45 years of age I pursued my certification as a Personal Trainer and love the art of body mechanics and the benefits of targeted exercise.

about me

my story

Most of my life I have been an active person. My passions in my youth were ballet and tap, but I have always loved various styles of dance. As I approached high school I progressed into sporting activities. Unfortunately for me, due to genetics and poor diet I began gaining weight. And of course this was at a time when you are young and vulnerable—the worst time for this to occur. I found the courage and strength to start making changes in my eating habits as well as increasing my exercise. As we read about our nation’s issues with childhood obesity — I share a very personal experience with this struggle.

my commitment

Having committed to a new change in lifestyle and in attaining my goal weight –I realized it was not enough. Through the process I had gained confidence and discipline…and I absolutely loved the new “Me!” (Not to mention my new body). I began a new journey—and ventured into the world of Resistance/Strength Training. I engaged a Personal Trainer for correct techniques in weights, strength and cardio training and the best way to attain over-all health and well being. This is where I became a huge enthusiast and knew that I would need to share my excitement!

my philosophy

flexibility

I simply have the ability and talent to provide passionate dedication to clients seeking optimum health! I have worked with clients from 12 to 75 years young! I have an added interest in the mature client and specialize in strength, flexibility and resistance training.

adopt

This is a key element in maintaining optimum health. The ability to keep my clients engaged in a program that they can repeat and reproduce to maintain optimal results.

support

I am an encouraging committed team player who takes great pride in supporting her clients through dedicated discipline. (Sometimes this can be as simple as a text message and others it can be that phone call to pull you in and give you a little boost)

time

It is the ultimate commitment to any program. The time required to make life changes is critical to success. As your coach and team mate I will dedicate my time and attention to supporting your goals.

Face to Face Training
All attention focused on you. This service is to provide a one-on-one customized workout plan, specifically to fit your fitness goals. During the sessions I will provide encouragement and coaching on form, weight training, flexibility, strength and balance.
Offsite & In-Home Training
For those of you who may be self conscious in a gym environment. Why put yourself through that when I am pleased to come to you and eliminate the stress. I will devise and implement a plan that works on your time frame and with your lifestyle in mind.
Group Packages
Why do this alone - when you can have the encouragement of friends and family working out with you and keeping you motivated.
Corporate Training Plans
At the corporate level — I will devise and implement a plan that works for you. I can be flexible and provide onsite training at your office or gym.
Toning Exercises
These sessions are for those who are looking to increase lean body mass and have strength and definition.
